Invirotel Pricing Structure

INVIROTEL has a flexible and creative approach for funding municipal and other utility metering projects. The only activity, which needs to be priced,
is the Pre-project plan Audit. After the Audit the extent, scope and time-lines of the project can be accurately estimated in order to ascertain real costs.
Funding proposal is therefore based on real on-site costs and time-lines.

The following funding models are available:

  • Total cost to Municipality.
  • Total funded solution paid over time from billed consumption and pre-paid vending.
  • Partially funded model. INVIROTEL can facilitate the funding of any projects of any scope and duration.
