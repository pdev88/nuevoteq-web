Concentrator

M320CTG - Standalone Bulk Meters With GPRS

  • Two way communication access to all units.
  • Indicates the electricity phase associated with every consumer.
  • Load control Programs.
  • Reports the power loading of each of the three phases.
  • Keeps the reports of the last ten power failures.
  • Accumulates 24 hour load curves for all the meters.
  • Informs immediately on a power failure by SMS.
  • Handles TOU.
  • Also functions as a Three-Phase check electricity meter.
